Time Complexity

The time complexity results from the Chiba and Nishizeki (1985) paper on arboricity and subgraph listing algorithms are:

  • Triangle Listing: O(α(G) ⋅ m)
  • Quadrangle (C4) Listing: O(α(G) ⋅ m)
  • Complete Subgraph Kt Listing: O(α(G)^(t-2) ⋅ m)
  • Clique Listing: O(α(G) ⋅ m) per clique.
